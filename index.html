Services

All of our services listed below are gender neutral and priced individually. Please contact us for a quote, or refer to our reservation page.

Highlights / Lowlights

Service provides highlights and/or lowlights depending on client need, varying from a full to a partial.

Hair Painting

Ranging from balayage to freehand painting, this service can provide a few pops of brightness or a whole head of dimension.

All Over Color

A simple one process color all over the hair, meant for a big change or to freshen up faded color over time.

Root Retouch

Touching up root color to continue grey coverage or for general color maintenance.

Lightener Retouch

Root touch up of lightener on the scalp. Must have only 1”-1 1/2” of regrowth to qualify as a lightener retouch.

Color Overhaul / Correction

Any kind of dramatic color change to the hair, charged hourly depending on client needs.

Long Cut

A cut on hair length below the ears, includes shampoo and style if requested

Short Cut

A cut on hair length above the ears, including shampoo and style if requested.

Style

A shampoo and style, including a blowout, silk press, or curls depending on client need. Charged hourly depending on technique.
